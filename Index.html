Chicken And Rice Dry Cat Food

Estimated time: 30 mins

Servings: 2 cats

Lasting: 3 days refrigerated

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked chicken (boiled or grilled, without bones)
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1/4 cup high-quality dry cat food
  • 1/4 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, and spinach)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (optional)

Instructions

  • Mash the cooked chicken and mix it with cooked rice.
  • Add the dry cat food and mixed vegetables.
  • Drizzle with olive oil (if using) and mix well.
  • Serve in small portions, refrigerating any leftovers for up to 3 days.

Mutton And Lentil Dry Cat Food

Estimated time: 30 mins

Servings: 2 cats

Lasting: 3 days refrigerated

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked mutton (boiled, without bones)
  • 1/2 cup cooked lentils (masoor dal)
  • 1/4 cup high-quality dry cat food
  • 1/4 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, and spinach)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (optional)

Instructions

  • Mash the cooked mutton and mix it with cooked lentils.
  • Add the dry cat food and mixed vegetables.
  • Drizzle with olive oil (if using) and mix well.
  • Serve in small portions, refrigerating any leftovers for up to 3 days.

Mutton And Sweet Potato Wet Cat Food

Estimated time: 45 mins

Servings: 2 cats

Lasting: 3 days refrigerated

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked mutton (boiled, without bones)
  • 1/2 cup cooked sweet potato (shakarkandi)
  • 1/4 cup high-quality dry cat food
  • 1/4 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, and spinach)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (optional)

Instructions

  • Mash the cooked mutton and mix it with cooked sweet potato.
  • Add the wet cat food and mixed vegetables.
  • Drizzle with olive oil (if using) and mix well.
  • Serve in small portions, refrigerating any leftovers for up to 3 days.

Local Fish And Rice Wet Cat Food

Estimated time: 45 mins

Servings: 2 cats

Lasting: 2 days refrigerated

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked local fish (basa, tilapia, or any other local fish)
  • 1/2 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1/4 cup wet cat food (canned or pouch)
  • 1/4 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, and spinach)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (optional)

Instructions

  • Mash the cooked fish and mix it with cooked rice.
  • Add the wet cat food and mixed vegetables.
  • Drizzle with olive oil (if using) and mix well.
  • Serve in small portions, refrigerating any leftovers for up to 2 days.
